About Us:

Welcome to Little Kidzee School, established in 2017, a nurturing haven for young minds located in the beautiful city of Manmad. With a legacy of nurturing excellence in the field of education for the last 6 years, the school is regarded as one of the most reputed English schools in Manmad. Incorporating traditional Indian values, basic life skills, and advanced technical tools makes the teaching-learning process at the school an enriching experience. Our goal is to create a friendly and exciting place for children to explore and grow. We want them to enjoy their journey of discovering new things and becoming their best versions.


At Little Kidzee School, we believe in fostering a love for learning from the very beginning. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to creating a safe and encouraging space for children to explore, inquire, and express themselves. Little Kidzee School is committed to providing an inclusive and diverse learning environment. We celebrate the uniqueness of each child, fostering a culture of acceptance and respect. Our classrooms are spaces where children from various backgrounds come together to learn, share, and grow, promoting a sense of unity and understanding. We recognize that each child is unique, and our tailored approach to education reflects our commitment to recognizing and nurturing individual strengths.

Vision:

We envision Little Kidzee School as a hub of creativity and innovation, where students are not just passive learners but active participants in their educational journey. Our vision is to empower every child with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to thrive in an ever-evolving global society.

Mission:

Our mission is to lay the foundation for a well-rounded education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also on the holistic development of each child. We aim to instill a lifelong love for learning, curiosity, and a sense of responsibility in our students.

English School:

Little Kidzee School is an English medium school, and we recognize the importance of proficiency in the English language for academic success and global communication. Our curriculum is designed to enhance language skills, ensuring that students develop strong reading, writing, and communication abilities.

Facilities:

Our school boasts modern facilities that contribute to a well-rounded educational experience. From well-equipped classrooms to interactive learning spaces, we provide an environment that encourages curiosity and creativity. Our dedicated staff is trained to provide a supportive atmosphere that promotes both academic and personal growth.

Parental Involvement:

We believe that a child's education is a collaborative effort between the school and parents. We encourage active participation from parents and guardians in their child's educational journey, fostering a sense of community and support. At Little Kidzee School, we are not just educators; we are facilitators of dreams and architects of bright futures. Join us as we embark on this exciting adventure of learning, laughter, and limitless possibilities. Together, let's build a strong foundation for your child's success and happiness.
